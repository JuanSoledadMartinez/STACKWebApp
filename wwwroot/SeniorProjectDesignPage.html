About

We’ve served this richly diverse region as a community hospital for more than 60 years. And as the area has grown, so have we. But as Long Island doctors, nurses, and healthcare professionals, we’ve never outgrown our devotion to the health of the community we all call home. That commitment can be seen in the Knapp Cardiac Care Center, a new surgical pavilion, our level III trauma center, our women’s imaging, breast health, orthopedics, bariatrics, and so much more. So while we’ve updated our brand to represent our growth and the patient-first changes we’ve made, there is one thing that will never change: Our commitment to make you feel as comfortable in our home as you do in your own.
